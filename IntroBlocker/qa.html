Questions and Answers

Q: What species is IntroBlocker suitable for?

A: IntroBlocker was designed based on wheat, but it could be used in major crops, such as rice, barley, soybean, etc.

Q: How could I deploy the IntroBlocker locally?

A: IntroBlocker was mainly implemented in python, so it could be used in the environment with a python installation.
