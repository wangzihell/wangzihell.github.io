Questions and Answers

Q: What genomes of species is GeneTribe suitable for?

A: GeneTribe takes advantage of collinear block scores and chromosome group information, and it is suitable for homology inference among polyploids that are genetically similar, such as Triticeae.

Q: Can I run GeneTribe based on pre-computed BLAST results?

A: Yes. GeneTribe can running based on pre-computed files.
1. Move files to directory DIR1.
2. Add the parameter -d DIR1 to command genetribe core.
